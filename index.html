About Jean

Jean Grey is a omega-level mutant on Earth-616. Born in New York, United States, Jean Grey now resides in Manhattan, where she works as the headmaster of the Xavier Institute for Mutant Education and Outreach. In her spare time, Jean has become interested in coding and programming, as a means to exercise her brain in ways that telepathy and telekinesis cannot.
